About the Fellowship

Equitable futures require
rooted infrastructure.

rooted/pwr is a 5-month learning and doing fellowship that responds to a well-documented gap: organizations serving Black and Latine/x communities are expected to deliver results quickly, equitably, and transparently - yet often operate fragmented project management systems with limited internal capacity and a lack of frameworks for implementation and sustainability.

The fellowship equips residents, community leaders, and organizations with the tools, support, and collective infrastructure needed not only to implement well, but to scale and endure well. It is not about solving one-off capacity gaps - it is about building the muscle memory for effective project management practices that can be sustained beyond any single person or grant period.

At its core, rooted/pwr represents our conviction that equitable and prosperous multigenerational futures are still possible - and that the path runs through shared power and disciplined implementation.
